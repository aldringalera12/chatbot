TYPES OF CROSS ENROLMENT:
Cross-enrolment at PRMSU has four types: Inbound, 
for students from other institutions taking specific courses at PRMSU; Outbound, for 
PRMSU students enrolling in courses at other institutions for credit; In-Campus, for 
students taking courses in other colleges within the same campus; and Out-Campus, for 
those enrolling in courses offered by other PRMSU campuses.
